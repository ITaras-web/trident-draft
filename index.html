about

With over 15 years of experience serving Dublin, Trident Constructions specializes in extensions, renovations, roofing, and decking that transform houses into dream homes. We pride ourselves on delivering exceptional quality at competitive prices, using only the finest materials and skilled craftsmanship. Our friendly, reliable team works closely with you from start to finish, helping plan every detail to bring your vision to life quickly and efficiently. Having completed 25+ projects across Dublin, we've had the privilege of helping families create spaces they truly love—and we're ready to help you build yours.

Services

roofing

From repairs to full replacements, we deliver durable roofing solutions using premium materials and expert craftsmanship to protect your home against the Irish weather.

Extension

From concept to completion, we craft stunning, functional home extensions that blend seamlessly with your existing space, enhancing comfort, value, and effortless everyday living.

Decking

We design and build durable, weather-resistant decking using premium materials, creating the perfect outdoor space for relaxing, entertaining, and enjoying every sunny season.

Renovation
